02 / BRAND ETHOS

CONSTRUCTING AN ARCHIVE OF EXPERIMENTAL UTILITY

Founded by designer Odafe, the brand is a physical exploration of architectural structure, industrial textures, and modern utility. Deconstructing traditional garments and rebuilding them with tactical details, raw raw-edged hems, and graphic typography, each piece serves as an armor for the contemporary landscape.

Every design is treated as a concept study, marrying complex sewing patterns and distressed graphic overlays (like the signature "Broken Record" and technical spec screenprints) with heavy, durable fabrics. The result is a bold, industrial collection that challenges conventional proportions.
