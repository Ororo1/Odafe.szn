02 / BRAND ETHOS

CONSTRUCTING AN ARCHIVE OF EXPERIMENTAL UTILITY

Founded by designer Odafe, the brand is a physical exploration of architectural structure, industrial textures, and modern utility. Deconstructing traditional garments and rebuilding them with tactical details, raw-edged hems, and graphic typography, each piece serves as an armor for the contemporary landscape.

Every design is treated as a concept study, marrying complex sewing patterns and distressed graphic overlays with heavy, durable fabrics. The result is a bold, industrial collection that challenges conventional proportions, from the motorsport fury of ODAFE V66, to the quiet cultural gravity of Ojú Pọn, to the crowned purpose of Mirch 1.0.
